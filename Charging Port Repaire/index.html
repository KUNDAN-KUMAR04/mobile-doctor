Common Charging Port Problems We Fix

Phone Not Charging

Your phone isn't charging at all when plugged in, indicating a complete failure of the charging port.

Intermittent Charging

The phone charges on and off, or only when the cable is held at a specific angle, suggesting a loose connection.

Loose or Damaged Port

The charging cable feels loose in the port, or there's visible damage like bent pins or debris inside.

Overheating During Charge

The charging port or surrounding area gets unusually hot during charging, which can be a sign of a short circuit.
