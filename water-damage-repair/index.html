Common Signs of Water Damage in Your Phone

If your phone has come into contact with liquid, look out for these common indicators of water damage:

  • No Power: Phone won't turn on or suddenly shuts down.
  • Display Issues: Flickering screen, discolored patches, lines, or no display at all.
  • Charging Problems: Phone not charging, or showing charging but not gaining power.
  • Audio Malfunctions: Distorted sound from speakers/earpiece, or no sound.
  • Corrosion: Visible corrosion on charging port or SIM tray.
  • Sensor Issues: Malfunctioning touch screen, camera, or other sensors.

Even if your phone seems fine initially, liquid damage can cause long-term corrosion. Act fast!

Our Expert Water Damage Restoration Process

Time is critical when dealing with water damage. Our streamlined process aims for quick and effective restoration:

  1. Immediate Disassembly: We quickly open your phone to disconnect the battery and prevent further short-circuiting.
  2. Thorough Cleaning: All internal components are meticulously cleaned using specialized solutions to remove corrosion and mineral deposits.
  3. Drying & Inspection: Components are professionally dried, and each part is inspected for damage.
  4. Component Replacement (If Needed): Any irreversibly damaged components are replaced with high-quality parts.
  5. Reassembly & Testing: The phone is reassembled, and extensive tests are performed to ensure full functionality.

Important: Do NOT try to turn on or charge a water-damaged phone. Bring it to us immediately!
